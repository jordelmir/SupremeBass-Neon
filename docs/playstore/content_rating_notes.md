# SupremeBass - Content Rating Notes

**Date:** July 4, 2026

## IARC Content Rating Questionnaire Responses

### Violence
**None** — The app contains no violent content, imagery, or themes. SupremeBass is a utility app for audio enhancement with no narrative or visual elements depicting violence.

### Fear
**None** — The app does not contain any content designed to cause fear, anxiety, or distress. No horror themes, jump scares, or threatening imagery.

### Language
**None** — The app contains no profanity, offensive language, or mature dialogue. All in-app text is neutral and professional.

### Sexual Content
**None** — The app contains no sexual content, nudity, or suggestive material. All visuals are abstract or UI-related.

### Controlled Substances
**None** — The app does not reference or depict the use of alcohol, tobacco, drugs, or controlled substances in any form.

### User Interaction
**None** — The app does not include user-to-user communication features such as chat, messaging, forums, or social sharing. There is no way for users to interact with each other within the app.

### Digital Purchases
**Planned Future** — The current version of SupremeBass is free and does not include in-app purchases. A premium version with additional features is planned for future release. At that time, this section will be updated to reflect in-app purchase capabilities.

### Location
**Not Shared** — The app does not collect or share user location data. No GPS, network-based, or approximate location data is accessed.

### Personal Information
**Not Collected** — The app does not collect personally identifiable information. No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other PII is gathered.

### User-Generated Content
**None** — The app does not allow users to create, share, or upload content.

### External Links
**None** — The app does not contain external links to websites or other applications.

## IARC Rating Determination

Based on the above questionnaire responses, SupremeBass qualifies for the following IARC rating:

**Rating:** Teen (T) or Everyone (E)
**Rating Bodies:** IARC (International Age Rating Coalition)

**Rationale:**
- No violent content
- No sexual content
- No profanity or mature language
- No controlled substance references
- No user interaction features
- No location sharing
- No sensitive personal data collection
- App is a utility tool for audio enhancement
