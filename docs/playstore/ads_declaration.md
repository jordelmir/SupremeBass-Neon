# SupremeBass - Ads Declaration

**App:** SupremeBass - Bass Booster & Sound Enhancer
**Developer:** Supreme Corp
**Date:** July 4, 2026

## Ad Disclosure

### Does this app contain ads?
**Yes**

---

## Ad Types

| Ad Type | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Banner Ads** | Displayed at the bottom of select screens. Non-intrusive format that allows continued app use while viewing. |
| **Interstitial Ads** | Full-screen ads shown at natural transition points within the app. |
| **Rewarded Ads** | Optional video ads that users may choose to watch in exchange for enhanced features or capabilities. |

## When Ads Are Shown

### Ads ARE displayed during:
- After saving a custom preset
- After applying a preset from the preset list
- Periodically during normal app usage (minimum 3-minute interval between interstitial ads)
- After completing certain app actions at natural break points

### Ads are NOT displayed during:
- Audio control adjustments (bass slider, EQ slider movements)
- Switch toggles (on/off settings)
- Active audio processing
- Initial app startup
- Settings screen navigation

---

## Ad Frequency

- **Interstitial ads:** Maximum of 1 interstitial ad every 3 minutes
- **Banner ads:** Persistent on designated screens, removed during active controls
- **Rewarded ads:** Only shown when user explicitly opts to watch

---

## User Control

Users can manage ad experience through:
- Device settings to opt out of personalized advertising
- App Settings screen for ad preferences
- Rewarded ads are always optional — users choose whether to watch

---

## Data Collection for Ads

Ad personalization uses:
- Advertising ID (resettable identifier)
- Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
- App interaction data (anonymized usage patterns)

No audio data, location data, or personal information is used for ad targeting.
