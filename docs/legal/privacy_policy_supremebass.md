## 1. Introduction

Supreme Corp ("we," "our," or "us") operates the SupremeBass mobile application (the "App"). ## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Collected Automatically

**Advertising ID**
- We use Google AdMob, which may collect your Advertising ID (AAID/GAID) for the purpose of delivering personalized advertisements. This is a resettable identifier provided by Google Play Services and is not tied to persistent device identifiers.

**App Interaction Data**
- We collect anonymized data about how you interact with the App, such as features used, session duration, and general usage patterns. This data is used to improve the App experience and for ad performance measurement.

**Device Information**
- Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, language settings, and general device characteristics. This information is collected by Google AdMob for ad serving and compatibility purposes.

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

SupremeBass does **not** collect:

- **Audio data** — All audio processing occurs locally on your device. No audio files, recordings, or sound data are transmitted from your device.
- **Microphone access** — The App does not request or use microphone permissions.
- **Contacts** — We do not access, collect, or store your contact list.
- **Location data** — The App does not request or use location permissions (GPS, network-based, or otherwise).
- **Personal files** — We do not access your personal files, photos, documents, or other stored data.
- **Music library** — We do not access your music library or media files.
- **Personally identifiable information** — We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other PII beyond what is described in this policy.
